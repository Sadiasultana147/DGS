Comprehensive Training for Elite Security Personnel

At Desh Guard Services (Pvt.) Ltd., we are committed to building a highly skilled and disciplined security force. Our training programs are designed to empower personnel with physical endurance, tactical knowledge, and ethical integrity to meet modern security challenges.

1. Physical Excellence

Our sessions focus on stamina, strength, and agility to prepare guards for demanding field duties through structured routines and endurance drills.

2. Tactical Expertise

From CCTV operation to access control and crisis response, we equip personnel with modern surveillance and emergency handling techniques.

3. Ethical Conduct

Legal literacy, professionalism, and responsible behavior are deeply embedded into our training philosophy, ensuring integrity in every action.

Core Modules Include:
  • Physical Fitness & Endurance: Strength and stamina building routines.
  • Advanced Surveillance Techniques: CCTV operation and situational alertness.
  • Access Control Management: Gatekeeping and verification processes.
  • Emergency Response: Fire safety, first aid, and disaster drills.
  • Legal & Ethical Conduct: Training in laws, rights, and duties.
  • Weapons & Self Defense: Authorized arms use and unarmed tactics.
  • Communication & Reporting: Two-way radio use, report writing, and incident logs.

Every course is delivered by certified, veteran trainers with real-world experience. Practical exercises, scenario-based simulations, and routine evaluations ensure every trainee becomes field-ready.

We also offer tailored refresher courses and corporate security training to meet specific operational demands and evolving security trends.

Discipline & Ethics

We instill strong discipline, moral conduct, and a sense of duty in every trainee.

Targeted Skill Training

We tailor sessions for physical fitness, crisis handling, guarding techniques, and more.

Certified Programs

Each successful trainee receives a certificate upon program completion.
