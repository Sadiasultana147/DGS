OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Security Solutions Across Bangladesh

Professional & Trusted Since 2013

DESH GUARD SERVICES PVT. LTD. has been providing reliable and professional security services to over 50 organizations since 2013. With more than 150 trained personnel, we serve embassies, NGOs, banks, factories, commercial and residential properties.

Our company is proud to be led by ex-defense professionals from ARMY, BDR, and ANSAR. We operate nationwide, with zonal offices in Chittagong and Sylhet, and a 24/7 Operational Control Room that ensures prompt response and continuous support.

Your Safety, Our Priority

At Desh Guard Services (Pvt.) Ltd., we believe that true security is achieved through a combination of unwavering vigilance, advanced strategy, and human excellence. Our elite security personnel are meticulously trained, background-verified, and equipped with the latest tools and protocols to meet evolving threats and client expectations.

Whether it’s corporate facilities, residential complexes, or special events, we deliver customized, proactive security solutions with precision and care. With us, safety is not just a service — it is a commitment to excellence, reliability, and peace of mind.

Our Security Solutions

  • Security Guard In Charge: Senior-most personnel at each post, ensuring discipline and shift coordination.
  • Security Supervisor: Conducts surprise visits and inspections to maintain service quality.
  • Security Commander: Manages multi-site deployments and acts as client liaison.
  • Security Guards (Male/Female): Uniformed, trained, and verified personnel for entrances, factories, homes, and events.
  • Building Manager: Oversees all facility operations, reporting, and staff discipline.
  • Building Care Tracker: Ensures the upkeep and hygiene of property infrastructure.
  • Cleaners (Male/Female): Professionally trained in industrial and residential cleaning protocols.

At Desh Guard Services (Pvt.) Ltd., we take pride in offering a comprehensive range of professional security solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our team consists of highly trained personnel with backgrounds in defense, paramilitary, and civilian sectors.
