Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our security and piyon services.

What types of security services do you provide?
We offer a wide range of services including unarmed and armed security guards, personal bodyguards, event security, industrial security, and piyon/messenger services tailored to client needs.
How do you train your security personnel?
All our personnel undergo rigorous training programs covering physical fitness, legal and ethical responsibilities, emergency response, communication skills, and client-specific protocols.
Can I hire a guard or piyon for short-term events?
Yes, we provide flexible contracts including short-term and event-specific security and piyon services to meet your unique requirements.
What is the process to request security services?
You can contact us via phone, email, or the contact form on our website. Our team will assess your needs and provide a tailored proposal including pricing and personnel deployment.
Are your security guards licensed and insured?
Yes, all our security personnel are fully licensed, vetted, and insured in compliance with local laws and industry standards.
