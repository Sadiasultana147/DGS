Our History

Desh Guard Services – A Legacy of Trust, Discipline & Service Since 2013

Company History & Background

Desh Guard Services (Pvt.) Ltd. was founded in 2013, originally operating as a locksmith company. Through strategic growth, visionary leadership, and a commitment to excellence, it has evolved into one of the most trusted and professional security service providers in Bangladesh.

With over a decade of operational experience, we have built a robust infrastructure and a reputation for reliability. Our services span a wide range of sectors including commercial, industrial, residential, and government facilities. We pride ourselves on our ability to adapt to dynamic client requirements while ensuring the highest level of security and professionalism.

Our strength lies in our people. Our team comprises highly trained professionals—many of whom are distinguished veterans from the ARMY, BDR, and ANSAR, as well as personnel from academic and technical backgrounds. This unique blend of experience and expertise enables us to deliver comprehensive security solutions tailored to the specific needs of each client.

We remain steadfast in our core values of trust, discipline, and consistent performance. At Desh Guard Services, we empower our clients with a sense of peace and protection by delivering dependable, transparent, and uncompromised security services—24/7, 365 days a year.

Over the years, Desh Guard Services has embraced innovation and technology, integrating state-of-the-art surveillance systems and training methodologies to stay ahead in the security landscape. We invest continuously in upgrading our resources and skillsets, ensuring that our clients receive cutting-edge security solutions that meet international standards.

Our commitment to corporate social responsibility and community engagement reflects in our ethical business practices and transparent client relationships. We believe in creating a safer Bangladesh, not only through security but also by fostering awareness and collaboration among stakeholders.

Looking forward, Desh Guard Services envisions expanding its footprint nationally and regionally, strengthening partnerships with both private and public sectors. We remain dedicated to excellence, adaptability, and integrity, forging long-term relationships built on trust and mutual success.
